# VeerNeo - AI-Powered 3D Model Generation

Transform ideas into 3D reality with VeerNeo, an advanced AI agent that generates editable 3D models from natural language prompts.

## About VeerNeo

VeerNeo is founded by **Garima** and **Nilesh** to revolutionize CAD development through artificial intelligence. Our cutting-edge AI technology enables professionals to:

- Generate complex 3D models from simple text descriptions
- Edit and customize generated models with intuitive controls
- Export models to any standard CAD format
- Accelerate the design workflow significantly

## How VeerNeo Works

1. **Describe** - Write a natural language description of your 3D model
2. **Generate** - Our AI processes your prompt and creates a high-quality model
3. **Edit** - Customize and refine the model to your specifications
4. **Export** - Download your model in any standard 3D format

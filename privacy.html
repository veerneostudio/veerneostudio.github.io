Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 09, 2026


1. Information We Collect

VeerNeo Studio collects minimal information required to provide our 3D generative services. This may include email addresses provided via contact forms and diagnostic data to improve our neural geometry engines.


2. How We Use Information

Data collected is used solely for service improvement and direct communication. We do not sell user data to third parties.


3. CAD Data Privacy

Geometric data generated through VeerNeo is processed securely. We respect the intellectual property of our users and do not claim ownership of user-prompted geometry.


4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at veerneostudio@gmail.com.
